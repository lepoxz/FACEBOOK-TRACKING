# Brand Identity

## Brand Vision

FACEBOOK TRACKING duoc dinh vi nhu mot "ban dieu khien tinh bao" danh cho team van hanh Facebook. Thuong hieu can truyen tai 4 dac tinh chinh:

- Chinh xac: giao dien va ngon ngu phai tao cam giac du lieu dang tin cay, khong khoa truong.
- Canh bao som: bat thuong, bien dong va co hoi can duoc nhin thay nhanh.
- Giao van hanh: uu tien quyet dinh tac nghiep hon la trinh dien marketing.
- Tinh gon: mat do thong tin cao nhung khong roi, de quet va hanh dong nhanh.

Cam xuc can goi ra la "kiem soat tinh hinh", khong phai "mot landing page dep". Moi quyet dinh thi giac phai phuc vu viec doc du lieu, so sanh doi thu va phat hien alert.

## Doi Tuong Su Dung

- Nhan su agency theo doi fanpage doi thu va tong hop bien dong.
- Nhan su ads/care can bat nhanh bai dang, livestream, muc do tuong tac va alert.
- Nguoi dung thao tac nhieu gio tren desktop; mobile chi uu tien xem nhanh va check canh bao.

## Thuoc Tinh Thuong Hieu

| Thuoc tinh | Mo ta | Nen the hien bang |
|-----------|-------|-------------------|
| Tactical | Giong mot cong cu chien dich | Sidebar ro rang, metric block gon, uu tien trang thai |
| Analytical | Co trong so du lieu | Kieu chu sach, contrast cao, card co cap bac thong tin |
| Responsive | Nhanh voi bien dong | Mau canh bao ro, CTA cap 1 noi bat |
| Disciplined | Dong nhat va co he thong | Token hoa mau sac, spacing, radius, shadow |

## Color Palette

| Role | Name | Hex | RGB | Usage |
|------|------|-----|-----|-------|
| Primary | Signal Teal | `#0F766E` | `15, 118, 110` | CTA chinh, trang thai dang theo doi, diem nhan chart |
| Secondary | Midnight Slate | `#0F172A` | `15, 23, 42` | Sidebar, tieu de lon, nen khu vuc dieu huong |
| Accent | Alert Amber | `#F59E0B` | `245, 158, 11` | Canh bao can chu y, badge bien dong, highlight timeline |
| Neutral | Cloud Mist | `#F8FAFC` | `248, 250, 252` | Nen tong, surface sang, khu vuc mo rong |
| Neutral | Steel Gray | `#475569` | `71, 85, 105` | Noi dung phu, label, note |
| Error | Critical Red | `#DC2626` | `220, 38, 38` | Alert muc do cao, loi form, metric giam manh |
| Success | Verified Green | `#16A34A` | `22, 163, 74` | Xac nhan thanh cong, he thong on dinh |

### Nguyen tac su dung mau

- `Midnight Slate` la mau neo thuong hieu, tao nen chuyen nghiep va tach biet khoi cac dashboard pastel thong thuong.
- `Signal Teal` la mau hanh dong chinh vi lien tuong den tracking, monitoring va tin hieu.
- `Alert Amber` chi dung cho cac diem can chu y, tranh dung lam mau trang tri.
- Nen du lieu uu tien trung tinh sang de bang bieu va table de doc lau.

## Typography

| Role | Typeface | Weight | Size | Usage |
|------|----------|--------|------|-------|
| Heading | Sora | 600, 700 | `32/40`, `24/32`, `20/28` | Page title, section heading, metric emphasis |
| Body | IBM Plex Sans | 400, 500, 600 | `14/20`, `16/24` | Table, form, navigation, body copy |
| Mono | IBM Plex Mono | 500 | `12/18`, `13/20` | Timestamp, page ID, metric delta, technical labels |

### Nguyen tac kieu chu

- `Sora` chi dung cho tieu de va KPI lon de tao chat cong cu hien dai.
- `IBM Plex Sans` la font chinh cho van hanh vi de doc o mat do thong tin cao.
- `IBM Plex Mono` chi xuat hien o du lieu co cau truc nhu ma trang, timestamp, chi so thay doi.

## Logo Usage

Tam thoi dung wordmark text `FACEBOOK TRACKING` cho giai doan MVP.

- Logo chinh: wordmark tren nen `Midnight Slate`, chu mau trang hoac `Cloud Mist`.
- Clear space toi thieu: bang chieu cao chu `F`.
- Kich thuoc toi thieu: 24px chieu cao trong app, 96px chieu rong trong tai lieu.
- Khong duoc:
  - them gradient vao wordmark
  - keo gian ngang/doc
  - dat logo len nen nhieu hoa tiet khong co lop dem
  - doi mau wordmark sang cac mau ngoai he token

## Iconography

- Phong cach: outline bo goc nhe, uu tien icon co hinh hoc ro rang.
- Stroke: `1.75px`.
- Grid: `20px` cho icon trong navigation va bang bieu, `24px` cho hero metric.
- Dau muc semantic:
  - theo doi/on dinh: teal
  - canh bao: amber
  - loi/nghiem trong: red
- Icon khong dung de trang tri. Moi icon phai bo tro scan thong tin hoac trang thai.

## Tone of Voice

Thuong hieu giao tiep bang Tieng Viet ngan, ro va thao tac duoc.

### Tinh cach ngon ngu

- Truc dien, khong khoa truong.
- Uu tien dong tu hanh dong.
- Luon noi ro doi tuong, muc do va thoi diem.

### Mau dung

- "Phat hien 3 bai dang moi trong 2 gio qua."
- "Tuong tac tang 42% so voi trung binh 7 ngay."
- "Can kiem tra page nay, tan suat livestream dang bat thuong."

### Mau khong dung

- "Bung no tuong tac cuc manh."
- "Day la co hoi vang khong the bo lo."
- "He thong dang rat tuyet voi."

## Anh xa vao san pham

- Sidebar va top navigation phai uu tien `Midnight Slate` va `Signal Teal`.
- Card metric, table, timeline va alert panel phai theo mot he thong surface nhat quan.
- Moi trang thai trong dashboard can co ten token va quy tac, khong dung mau tuy y.
